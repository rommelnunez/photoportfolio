Rommel Nunez is a portrait photographer with 4 years of experience. He regularly shoots with musical artists of the likes of Eva Westphal, Christina Li, and Wander Marchal. His work has been published in The Columbia Daily Spectator, Ratrock Magazine, amongst others.

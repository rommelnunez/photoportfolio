Rommel Nunez is a portrait photographer from Tamaulipas, Mexico based in New York City. He is a student of Computer Science pursuing a minor in English & Comparative Literautre at Columbia University. Rommel regularly shoots with musical artists of the likes of Eva Westphal, Christina Li, and Wander Marchal. He has shot product photography for Salvos, Guayaki and his work has been published in The Columbia Daily Spectator, Ratrock Magazine, amongst others.
